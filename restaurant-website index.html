✦ Our Story

Passion on Every Plate

Founded in 2012, Ember & Oak was born from a simple belief — food should be more than just fuel. It should be an experience that brings people together, tells a story, and lingers long after the last bite.

Our head chef brings 20 years of culinary expertise, combining traditional techniques with modern creativity to craft dishes that are both comforting and surprising.
